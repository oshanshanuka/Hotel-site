Jetwing Lighthouse

Jetwing Lighthouse in Galle, Sri Lanka, is a luxurious five-star hotel known for its colonial charm, stunning coastal views, and exceptional design. Here’s a closer look at what it offers:

See more Jetwing Lighthouse photos

1. Location & Architecture

  • Overlooks the Indian Ocean, near Galle Fort.
  • Designed by architect Geoffrey Bawa, blending colonial and modern aesthetics.

2. Accommodations

  • Elegant rooms with dark wood furnishings and sea views.
  • Suites like the “Bawa Room” blend colonial charm with modern amenities.

3. Dining

  • Cardamom Café: Serves international and local cuisine.
  • Anchor Bar & Grill: Seafood and casual dining by the pool.
