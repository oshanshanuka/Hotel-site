Service

Dine & Relax
DINING

Restrurent

Jetwing Hotels offers a remarkable dining experience that celebrates Sri Lankan culinary heritage while embracing global flavors. With a focus on fresh, locally sourced ingredients, their restaurants create innovative dishes that cater to diverse tastes. Guests can enjoy everything from authentic Sri Lankan curries to exquisite international cuisine.


The dining spaces at Jetwing Hotels are as impressive as the cuisine, featuring elegant interiors and stunning natural backdrops. Whether it's a beachfront dinner, a rooftop feast, or a serene garden setting, each meal becomes a memorable experience. Jetwing seamlessly combines culinary artistry with warm hospitality, creating a dining journey to savor.

RELAX

Spa & Wellness

Jetwing Spa is a sanctuary of relaxation, offering rejuvenating therapies that are inspired by ancient Sri Lankan traditions and global wellness practices. Nestled in serene settings, each spa experience is designed to restore balance and rejuvenation to the body and mind, using natural ingredients and holistic techniques for a truly revitalizing escape.


From calming massages to invigorating body scrubs and signature treatments, Jetwing Spa ensures personalized care tailored to individual needs. Combined with tranquil ambiance and expert therapists, the spa provides a haven of peace, allowing guests to unwind completely and reconnect with their inner well-being.

DRINKS

Roof Top Bar

The rooftop bars at Jetwing Hotels offer a perfect blend of sophistication and breathtaking views. Perched high above, these stylish venues provide a serene escape where guests can enjoy signature cocktails, fine wines, and an array of refreshing beverages. Each drink is crafted to elevate the rooftop experience.
