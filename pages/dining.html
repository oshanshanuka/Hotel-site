Dining at Jetwing Hotels

Discover exceptional culinary experiences across our dining venues at Jetwing Hotels, each offering unique flavors, fresh ingredients, and unforgettable settings.

Dining Venues

  • Cardamom Café: An all-day dining venue that serves an array of international and Sri Lankan cuisine, offering both buffet and à la carte options.
  • Anchor Bar & Grill: Located by the pool, this casual venue specializes in seafood, light snacks, and refreshing cocktails for a relaxed dining experience.
  • The Verandah: A fine dining experience with breathtaking views, focusing on traditional Sri Lankan flavors with a modern twist.
  • In-Room Dining: Enjoy gourmet meals in the privacy of your own room with our extensive in-room dining menu, available 24/7.
