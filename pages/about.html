About Us

Jetwing Hotels in Sri Lanka is a premier hospitality brand renowned for its luxurious accommodations, impeccable service, and commitment to sustainability. Established over four decades ago, Jetwing offers a diverse portfolio of properties ranging from beachfront resorts to serene hilltop retreats. Each hotel reflects the rich cultural heritage of Sri Lanka while providing modern amenities and personalized experiences. Jetwing is also a leader in eco-tourism, implementing innovative green practices like renewable energy use and water conservation. Guests can indulge in authentic Sri Lankan cuisine, rejuvenating spas, and breathtaking landscapes, making Jetwing Hotels an ideal choice for travelers seeking relaxation and adventure.
